Who Are We?

At Jose & Sons Construction, we embody a narrative woven with the threads of family values and a profound dedication to the art of construction.

Founded by Jose Cabrera, an Ecuadorian immigrant with an impressive track record of over two decades in the construction industry, collaborating with major construction firms, individuals, and the NYC government, our journey is a testament to the fusion of Ecuadorian craftsmanship and a decade-long wealth of industry expertise.

As the torchbearers of Jose & Sons General Construction, we take pride in being your reliable contractor, specializing in a diverse array of projects ranging from residential and commercial to industrial endeavors.

Our commitment to excellence is unwavering, and from the conceptualization stage to project completion, we prioritize quality, efficiency, and, above all, client satisfaction.

Choose us for a construction experience that transcends boundaries, turning visions into tangible reality. Connect with us today to embark on a journey of top-notch construction services tailored to meet your unique needs.
